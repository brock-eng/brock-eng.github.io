About

Meet Zach

Actively writing code, creating, composing, and more.

Thought-filled multidisciplinary engineer who believes that consistent daily progress is the life-blood that sustains incredible accomplishments.

Zach has worked a variety of positions, having worked in the construction industry for several years before transisitioning into tech. He holds a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Wisconsin - Platteville but somewhere along the way he found a previously undiscovered passion for code and software. Zach currently puts his diverse skillset to use as a Manufacturing Engineer for Seats Incorporated out of Reedsburg, WI.

Outside of work, Zach is a prolific percussionist and avid participant in the marching arts, having spent several seasons as a participant of Drum Corps International. Since then, he's worked with several Wisconsin area high school marching band programs where he inspires excellence in youth. If he's not hacking on a pad or drumset, Zach can be seen at the gym, reading a good book, or biking through local trails.
